Application Process

Joining LuxRide Inc as a valued team member is a straightforward process. We value professionalism and commitment to excellence across all roles. Please review the following steps to become a part of our team:

  • Step 1: Application Submission - Fill out the application form below with accurate details.
  • Step 2: Stats and Background Check - Provide a screenshot of your in-game stats and consent to a background check if applying for a driver role.
  • Step 3: Interview - Successful applicants will be invited for a short Discord interview to discuss your application and suitability.
  • Step 4: Onboarding - Upon passing all checks, you'll be onboarded and ready to start your journey with LuxRide Inc.

Team Member Requirements (General)

To maintain our standard of excellence, all LuxRide team members must meet certain requirements. Please ensure you meet the following criteria before applying:

  • Professional in-game appearance and excellent customer service skills are expected for all roles.
  • Minimum age of 25 years old in-game for driver roles. (Other roles may have different age requirements)
  • Willingness to adhere to LuxRide Inc's operational guidelines and service standards.
  • Valid driver's license and clean driving record in the game (For driver roles).
